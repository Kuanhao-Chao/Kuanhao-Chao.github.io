I release **OpenSpliceAI**, an open-source PyTorch framework for splice-site prediction. The [preprint](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.03.20.644351), [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Kuanhao-Chao/OpenSpliceAI), [documentation](https://ccb.jhu.edu/openspliceai/), and [slides](https://storage.googleapis.com/storage.khchao.com/slides/JHU_joint_lab_meeting_2024.pdf) are available.
