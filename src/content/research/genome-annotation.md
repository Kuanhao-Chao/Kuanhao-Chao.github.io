Accurate gene annotation is the foundation of genome biology. **LiftOn** uses graph-based methods to stitch together fragmented DNA and protein alignments, combining their complementary signals into more accurate annotations when transferring genes from one assembly to another.

LiftOn is published in [Genome Research (2025)](https://doi.org/10.1101/gr.279620.124) with [documentation and tutorials](https://ccb.jhu.edu/lifton/).
