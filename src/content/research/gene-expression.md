Sequence-to-function models can learn how regulatory DNA shapes gene expression. **Shorkie** is a masked-DNA language model pretrained on 165 fungal genomes and fine-tuned on high-resolution yeast transcription-factor induction time-course RNA-seq data. It predicts dynamic expression patterns in budding yeast and unifies ChIP-exo DNA-binding and RNA-seq coverage predictions within a single multi-species fungal model.

This work was carried out with the Kelley Lab at Calico Life Sciences.
