RNA splicing is one of the most intricate steps of gene expression. I built a deep, dilated residual convolutional neural network to decode the complexities of splicing, alternative splicing, and the effect of genetic variants on cryptic splicing.

The work is released as two open-source tools: **Splam**, a compact splice-site predictor that improves spliced alignments ([Genome Biology, 2024](https://doi.org/10.1186/s13059-024-03379-4)), and **OpenSpliceAI**, an efficient, modular re-implementation of SpliceAI that enables easy retraining on non-human species ([eLife, 2025](https://doi.org/10.7554/eLife.107454.3)).
