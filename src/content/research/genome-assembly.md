Reference genomes are biased toward a small number of individuals. I assembled and annotated **Han1**, the first gapless, reference-quality, fully annotated genome from a Southern Han Chinese individual, using PacBio HiFi and Oxford Nanopore long reads with the T2T-CHM13 assembly as a guide.

Published in [G3: Genes, Genomes, Genetics (2023)](https://doi.org/10.1093/g3journal/jkac321); the assembly is available from [NCBI](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/datasets/genome/GCA_024586135.1/).
