Wheeler graphs are an elegant framework for indexing many genomes at once, but deciding whether a graph *is* a Wheeler graph is NP-complete. I applied new renaming heuristics together with an SMT solver to make Wheeler-graph recognition computationally feasible, and built the **Wheeler Graph Toolkit (WGT)** for recognizing, visualizing, and generating Wheeler graphs.

Published in [iScience (2023)](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.isci.2023.107402); a collaboration between Johns Hopkins and UC Berkeley.
